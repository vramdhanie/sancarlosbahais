## What is Ridvan?

Ridvan (pronounced "riz-van") means "Paradise" in Arabic and marks the period when Bahá'u'lláh, the Founder of the Bahá'í Faith, declared His mission. It's a time of joy, unity, and celebration for Bahá'ís around the world.
