We're thrilled to announce the formation of a new study circle focused on community building and consultation!

## About the Study Circle

This study circle will explore Bahá'í concepts of building vibrant communities, the art of consultation, and applying spiritual principles to our daily lives. The materials are designed to be accessible to all, whether you're new to the Bahá'í Faith or have been studying for years.

## Study Topics

We'll be covering:
- The concept of community and its role in human progress
- The practice of consultation as a tool for decision-making
- How to apply spiritual principles in our neighborhoods
- Building unity through diversity

## When and Where

**When:** Every other Wednesday evening  
**Time:** 7:00 PM - 8:30 PM  
**Location:** Community Center (address provided upon registration)

## Who Can Join?

Everyone is welcome! The study circle materials are designed to be inclusive and thought-provoking regardless of background or belief system.

## How to Join

If you're interested in participating, please reach out to us by email or through our contact form. We'd be happy to provide more information and answer any questions you might have.

Looking forward to learning together!
