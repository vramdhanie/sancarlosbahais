We're excited to announce our upcoming Spring Neighborhood Service Project! On Saturday, March 20th, we'll be gathering at the community park to clean up and beautify our shared spaces.

## Event Details

**Date:** Saturday, March 20, 2024  
**Time:** 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM  
**Location:** San Carlos Community Park

## How to Participate

All are welcome to join us! We'll provide:
- Gloves and cleaning supplies
- Refreshments
- A wonderful opportunity to serve our community together

## What We'll Be Doing

- Picking up litter and debris
- Clearing pathways
- Planting flowers and shrubs
- General park maintenance

This is a great opportunity to connect with neighbors while making a positive impact on our community. Children and families are especially welcome!

## RSVP

Please let us know if you plan to attend so we can prepare accordingly. Contact us at [contact@sancarlosbahai.org](mailto:contact@sancarlosbahai.org) or call us directly.

We hope to see you there!
